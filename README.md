# FinPin

Marketplace for buying and selling digital goods, services, accounts, software, etc.

**Core features (current / planned):**
- Seller & buyer registration & profiles
- Product listings with description, price, screenshots
- Cart and checkout
- Secure payments via Stripe (or other methods)
- Escrow / transaction protection
- Dispute system & refunds
- Seller payouts (automatic / manual)
- Anti-fraud measures (new account limits, moderation)
- Personal account with order history & balance
- Legal pages: Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, Refund Policy, etc.

Open-source project — feel free to contribute, fork and adapt for your needs.
